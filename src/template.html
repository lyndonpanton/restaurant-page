About Our Restaurant

This restaurant has been open for 100 years and counting! We are located at a lovely venue in the middle of the city with a view of the seaside, three volcanoes, and a desert! At this establishment you can feast on a variety of dishes from across the globe, from the Efo Riro of Nigeria and the Pakoras of India to the Rouladen of Germany and the Farofa of Brazil!
